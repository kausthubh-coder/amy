Amy 1.0.7 refines the daily notes surface, capture sheet, stats, settings, and widgets.

- Today now keeps notes and calories aligned in one scrolling log surface under floating header controls.
- Food and label photo capture now uses one cleaner photo panel with clearer preview and accessibility states.
- Stats now shows daily averages against goals instead of week totals compared to daily goals.
- Goals & Targets now shows goal type, macro targets, saved/pending values, and safer input validation.
- Local Data now has clearer private backup export/import controls.
- The mini Today widget is now quick-log focused and supports vertical resize metadata.
