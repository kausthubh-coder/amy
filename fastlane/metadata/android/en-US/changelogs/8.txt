Amy 1.0.6 fixes daily logging, widgets, and stats polish.

- Photo and prompt captures now add a visible note line while logging calories.
- Streaks now show current contiguous streak separately from total logged days.
- Stats and Streaks use a cleaner single header with the two tabs at the top.
- Streaks now show today eaten, week total, and macro totals against goals.
- Macro progress circles now render real progress with distinct macro colors.
- Android widgets now use consistent fire calorie language and improved resize metadata.
